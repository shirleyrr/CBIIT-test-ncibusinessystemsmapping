Acquisitions and Contract Management:
Investments in systems used to support source selection, purchasing, tracking, and performance of contracted goods and services.
 		  eContracts, NCI OA Acquisition Portal, OA Intranet, Task Order Request for Proposal (TORFP-EZ) AC
EA, Capital Planning, IT Management & Oversight:
Investments in establishing and performing federal IT management responsibilities and initiatives such as IT Capital Planning & Investment Control (CPIC), Enterprise Architecture (EA) management, Project/Program Management (P/PM), and other IT management responsibilities. 		  Beacon, M-Files, PX EA
Extramural Research Management and Support:
Investments in all Extramural Grant support systems that are more administrative in nature, and not included in Grants Portfolio Management, Grants Financial Administration, and Application Assignment and Review. 		  RPG, ToGo, CRCHD Data Management System GR
Facilities and Logistics Operations and Management:
Investments in systems used in the ongoing management and maintenance of NIH physical assets as it pertains to the maintenance, environmental control, administration, certification and operation of office buildings, fleets, machinery, and other assets that are possessions of the Federal Government.  This includes physical security. 		  Purchasing Online Tracking System (POTS) FL
Financial Management:
Investments in systems used to support NIH's financial management and accounting processes as it pertains to the accounting, funds control, payments, collections and receivables, asset and liability management, reporting, and cost accounting of NIH financial resources.   		  FFRDC Invoice System, FAM, CONG2e, Sofie FM
Human Resources Management:
Investments in systems used to manage and process all professional and administrative information associated with "NIH people" related to their execution of NIH's mission.  This includes payroll, training, employee directory, non-scientific loan repayment, etc. 		  NCI Office of Workforce Management (OWM) HR Actions Electronic Tracking Tool, Edie HR
Information Security & Assurance:
General program investments for securing NIH information assets, including security management systems. 		    IS
NIH Business Administration and Management Support:
Investments in systems and software used to facilitate the general oversight, monitoring, management, and performance of NIH business and administrative operations.  This includes managing official executive communications; policy, guidance, and regulations; and public affairs. 		  DocMan, BIS (ERP), NCI Application Development and Support, NCiConnect B
Property Management:
Investments in systems used to track and control information related to the ownership and location of procured assets and resources with regard to quantity, quality, and location. 		    PM
