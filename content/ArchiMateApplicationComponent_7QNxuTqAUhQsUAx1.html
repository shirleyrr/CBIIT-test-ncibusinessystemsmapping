 C-RADS ArchiMate Diagram : ArchiMate Diagram

According to the questionnaire as of 12/8/2018, it appears this is a standalone application that data is manually entered.  It is not quite known what applications are used to access this component's database.  But it appears that C-RADS is not connected to any other business system.

 

From the questionnaire:  "Provides registered users the ability to search for indirect rate information on a specific company by name, EIN or State. The search results will provide information on negotiated rate agreements with the company, as well as information on any indirect cost rate proposals currently under review by Division of Financial Advisory Services (DFAS) and the responsible negotiator’s name and email address."
