07/15/2019 interview with Michael Guimond at NIH campus, building 31, 2C19

 

The NIH Ethics Management Information System (EMIS) is used by the NIH Ethics Community to track all ethics actions and to provide a means to report on the data.  

https://ethics.od.nih.gov/EMIS2/EMIS2-Help.htm

 

EMIS is built on .net 2.5, but has been compiled using .net 4.0

 

A rewrite of EMIS is targeted for 2010 or 2021 written in .net Core

 

 

https://ethics.od.nih.gov/procedures.htm
