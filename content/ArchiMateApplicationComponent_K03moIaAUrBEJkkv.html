Business Function Primary Text NIH Business Administration and Management Support - B
Business Function Secondary Text The nVision Automated Registration System allows users to view all nVision offerings along with permission details. Via this application, users can request access to data and unique privileges. The interface communicates with both approvers and registrants via email to streamline and automate the process. Approvers can approve or deny requests real-time. Built in tracking keeps a history of requests, approvals, justifications, and registration alterations (i.e. the adding or subtracting of elements). The application communicates with the user, updating them on registration status and providing instructions on accessing key business areas and applications once registration requests are granted.
